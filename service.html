Our Services

Crowns Bridges

Both crowns and most bridges are fixed prosthetic devices. Unlike removable devices such as dentures, which you can take out and clean daily, crowns and bridges are cemented onto existing teeth or implants, and can only be removed by a dentist. Crowns and bridges are available at affordable price ranges.

Implants

A dental implant is used to support one or more false teeth. It is a titanium screw that can replace the root of a tooth when it fails. We provide specialisied Implant planning Using advanced imaging modalities like CBCT. We provide complete oral rehabilitation treatments using dental implants (All on 4 / All on 6 Implant Rehabilitations).

Teeth Whitening

Tooth whitening lightens teeth and helps to remove stains and discoloration. Whitening is among the most popular cosmetic dental procedures. Make every teeth shine with regular dental cleaning and polishing. We provide advanced Flap surgery procedures for swelling and bleeding gums.

Root Canals

A root canal is a treatment used to repair and save a tooth that is badly decayed or becomes infected. Experience the change in pace of dentistry with our advanced Root canal Equipment (Endomotor). We provide single sitting Root canal treatments and apicoectomy procedures.

Wisdom Teeths

A wisdom tooth extraction is a surgical procedure to remove one or more of your wisdom teeth. We are specialised in painless surgical proceedures, impacted teeth removal, Facial trauma (fractures) treatments.

Braces

Dental braces are devices used to correct crowded or crooked teeth, or a misaligned jaw, known as malocclusion. Correct your mal-aligned or irregula teeth with Clips/Braces/Orthodontic treatment. We provide advanced Orthodontic treatments like Teeth coloured braces, Invisible braces.
